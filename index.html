Controls:

  • Rotate: Use a single finger on a touchscreen or the left click on your mouse
  • Zoom: Use two fingers on a touchscreen or the wheel on your mouse

Notes:

This viewer was made to load and explore files in the OFF file format, e.g. as generated by Antiprism. Kudos to Adrian Rossiter and Roger Kaufman. If you want to see more please visit this more advanced viewer.
